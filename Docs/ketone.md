Ketone pathway (fires whenever CBG >12)

| Ketone result | Action | Source |
|---|---|---|
| >0.6 mmol/L | Elevated → review by medical team, refer DIT. | JBDS §9, §11 |
| Ketonaemia >3 mmol/L **or** ketonuria >2+ | Consider FRIII to suppress ketones; refer **urgently** to DIT/on-call; investigate DKA (JBDS 02). | JBDS §9; UHL §2.3 |
